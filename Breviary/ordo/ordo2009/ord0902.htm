At the end of Vespers, after the V. Benedicamus Domino and its Respond are added 2 Alleluias, which is thenceforth omitted until Holy Saturday

After Vespers on this day at the Opening Versicles of each Hour is said Laus tibi Domine, Rex æternæ gloriæ instead of Alleluja.  This is said until Maundy Thursday exclusive.

In the temporal Office until Easter is omitted the Te Deum and in its place is said a ninth (or third) Respond.  Lauds is said according to the second scheme.  At Sunday Prime instead of Confitemini are said the Psalms Dominus regnavit and Jubilate, and on weekdays the fourth Psalm is added as noted in the Psalter.

At ferial Vespers the Ant. on Magnif is proper.

24

 Tue


St Matthias, Ap
Double II Class

Prime,
Martyrology : Announcement of Ash Wednesday from table of movable feasts

Vespers
Of the Feast

Throughout Lent, at the Office of the Season (except for the 4th Sunday in Lent), the Altar is not decorated with flowers, and the Organ is not played.

Throughout Lent, at the Ferial Office, (a) Antiphons on the Benedictus and Magnificat with their daily Collects are proper; (b) the Ferial Preces are said kneeling at all the Hours; (c) at Prime, the 4th Psalm is said as noted in the Psalter.

The following day, Ash Wednesday, is a privileged greater Feria, on which no feast may be kept; I and II Class Doubles are transferred, other Feasts commemorated according as the Rubrics permit; but the subsequent Ferias, until the Saturday after Passion Sunday inclusive, are unprivileged greater Ferias, which permit the keeping of all Feasts except Simples, with Commemoration of the Feria, but of Simples, a Commemoration is made in the ferial Office.
