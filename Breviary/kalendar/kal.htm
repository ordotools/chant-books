The Universal Kalendar
The list of Feastdays for the Universal Church are found in the Universal Kalendar. 

The Kalendar of the Church prescribes the dates set aside for the celebration of feast days of the Western Church throughout any given year, without taking into account feasts proper to a particular nation, diocese, or religious order, or movable feasts such as Sundays and feasts based on the Proper of the Season.
