The Way of the Cross

Prayers before the High Altar

In the Name of the Father, † and of the Son, and of the Holy Ghost. Amen.

Let us pray.

ASSIST us mercifully with thy help, O Lord God of our salvation: that we may enter with joy upon the meditation of those mighty acts, whereby thou hast given unto us life and immortality. Through thy Son, Jesus Christ our Lord, who liveth and reigneth with thee in the unity of the Holy Ghost, ever one God world without end. Amen.
V. O God, † make speed to save us.
R. O Lord, make haste to help us.

Officiant:  All praise be unto thee, O Lord, King of glory everlasting.
HYMN

BY the Cross her station keeping,
Stood the mournful Mother weeping,
Where he hung, her dying Son.

Through her soul , of joy bereavèd,
Torn with anguish, deeply grievèd,
Lo! The piercing sword hath run.
