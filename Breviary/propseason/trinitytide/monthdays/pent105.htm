Fifth Sunday of October

Semidouble

October 26th, 27th, or 28th

NOTE: That the Feast of Christ the King is kept on the last Sunday of October, which same is sometimes the Fourth and sometimes the Fifth Sunday.

1st Vespers
 

 

 

Vespers on the Eve of the
Fifth Sunday of October

The first part of Vespers is in the Ordinary

The Psalms, Antiphons, Chapter and Hymn are from the Psalter.

V.  Vespertína orátio ascéndat ad te, Dómine.
R.  Et descéndat super nos misericórdia tua.

V.  Let our evening prayer come up before thee, O Lord.
R.  And let thy mercy come down upon us.

Ad Magnif. Ant:  Tua est poténtia, * tuum regnum, Dómine: tu es super omnes gentes: da pacem, Dómine, in diébus nostris.

Ant. on Magnif:  Thine, O Lord, is the kingdom * and thou art exalted as head above all: give peace in our time, O Lord our God.
