Si Vesperæ sint de Feria, ad Magnificat dicitur ultima Antiphona ex praecedentibus Feriis prætermissa; alioquin, si omnes recitatæ fuerint, sumitur de Psalterio. If Vespers be of the Feria, the Antiphon at the Magnificat is the most recent of the Antiphons of the preceding days that was omitted. Otherwise, if all the preceding Antiphons have been said, it is taken from the Psalter.

In practice, however, this can never actually happen because, in order for Vespers to be of the Feria on Friday, both Friday and Saturday would have to not have feasts of semidouble rank or higher. However, if this were to happen, Saturday would be the office of Our Lady on Saturday, and in that case Friday Vespers would be First Vespers of Our Lady on Saturday.
