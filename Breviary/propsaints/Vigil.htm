Commemorations for Vigils
 

Monday

Ant:  Benedíctus, * Dóminus, Deus Israël, quia visitávit et liberávit nos.

Ant:  Blessed be * the Lord God of Israel, for he hath visited and redeemed his people.
V.  Repléti sumus mane misericórdia tua.
R.  Exsultávimus, et delectáti sumus.		 V.  O satisfy us early with thy mercy.
R.  That we may rejoice and be glad.

Tuesday
Ant:  Eréxit nobis * Dóminus cornu salútis in domo David, púeri sui. Ant:  Unto us * hath the Lord raised up an horn of salvation in the house of his servant David.
V.  Repléti sumus mane misericórdia tua.
R.  Exsultávimus, et delectáti sumus.		 V.  O satisfy us early with thy mercy.
R.  That we may rejoice and be glad.

Wednesday

Ant:  De manu ómnium, * qui odérunt nos, liberávit nos Dóminus.

Ant:  From the hand of all * that hate us, good Lord, deliver us.
V.  Repléti sumus mane misericórdia tua.
R.  Exsultávimus, et delectáti sumus.		 V.  O satisfy us early with thy mercy.
R.  That we may rejoice and be glad.

Thursday
Ant:  In sanctitáte * serviámus Dómino, et liberábit nos ab inimícis nostris. Ant:  Let us serve the Lord * in holiness, and he will deliver us out of the hands of our enemies.
V.  Repléti sumus mane misericórdia tua.
R.  Exsultávimus, et delectáti sumus.		 V.  O satisfy us early with thy mercy.
R.  That we may rejoice and be glad.

Friday

Ant:  Per víscera misericórdiæ * Dei nostri visitávit nos Oriens ex alto.

Ant:  Through the tender mercy * of our God, the Day-Spring from on high hath visited us.
V.  Repléti sumus mane misericórdia tua.
R.  Exsultávimus, et delectáti sumus.		 V.  O satisfy us early with thy mercy.
R.  That we may rejoice and be glad.

Saturday

Ant:  Illúmina, Dómine, * sedéntes in ténebris et umbra mortis, et dírige pedes nostros in viam pacis.

Ant:  Give light, O Lord, * to them that sit in darkness and in the shadow of death, and guide our feet into the way of peace.
V.  Repléti sumus mane misericórdia tua.
R.  Exsultávimus, et delectáti sumus.		 V.  O satisfy us early with thy mercy.
R.  That we may rejoice and be glad.
