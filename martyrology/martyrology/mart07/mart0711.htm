Announcement of any movable feasts

Romæ sancti Pii Primi, Papæ et Mártyris; qui martyrio coronátus est in persecutióne Marci Aurélii Antoníni.

At Rome, Pope Pius I, who was crowned with martyrdom in the persecution of Marcus Aurelius Antoninus.

Bérgomi sancti Joánnis Epíscopi, qui, ob tuéndam cathólicam fidem, ab Ariánis occísus est.

At Bergamo, St. John, a bishop, who was killed by the Arians for defending the Catholic faith.

Sidæ, in Pamphylia, sancti Cindéi Presbyteri, qui, sub Diocletiáno Imperatóre et Stratoníco Præside, post multa torménta, injéctus in ignem et nil læsus, demum in oratióne réddidit spíritum.

At Sida in Pamphylia, St. Cindeus, priest, in the time of Emperor Diocletian and the governor Stratonicus.  After suffering many torments, he was thrown into the fire, but was not injured by it.  He later yielded up his soul in prayer.

Córdubæ, in Hispánia, sancti Abúndii Presbyteri, qui, in persecutióne Arábica, cum in Mahumétis sectam inveherétur, martyrio coronátus est.

At Cordova in Spain, St. Abundius, a priest, crowned with martyrdom while preaching against the sect of Mohammed.

Nicópoli, in Arménia, natális sanctórum Mártyrum Januárii et Pelágiæ, qui, equúleo, úngulis et testárum fragméntis per dies quátuor cruciáti, martyrium implevérunt.

At Nicopolis in Armenia, the birthday of the holy martyrs Januarius and Pelagia, who for four days were racked, torn with iron claws and pieces of earthenware, and thus achieved their martyrdom.

In território Senonénsi sancti Sidrónii Mártyris.

In the territory of Sens, St. Sidronius, martyr.

Icónii, in Lycaónia, sancti Marciáni Mártyris, qui, sub Perénnio Præside, per multa torménta pervénit ad palmam.

At Iconium in Lycaonia, St. Marcian, martyr.  He obtained the palm of martyrdom by many torments, under the governor Perennius.

Bríxiæ sanctórum Mártyrum Savíni et Cypriáni.

At Brescia, the holy martyrs Savinus and Cyprian.

In território Pictaviénsi sancti Sabíni Confessóris.

In the territory of Poitiers, St. Sabinus, confessor.

Et álibi aliórum plurimórum sanctórum Mártyrum et Confessórum, atque sanctárum Vírginum.
R.  Deo grátias.

And elsewhere in divers places, many other holy martyrs, confessors, and holy virgins.
R.  Thanks be to God.
